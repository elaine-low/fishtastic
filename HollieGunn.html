Dr Hollie Gunn

BVetMed, MRCVS

Dr. Gunn graduated from the Royal Veterinary College in London and has since worked in the United Kingdom, Australia and South Africa. Before moving to Singapore, she ran her own company in the UK, providing locum services to several state-of-the-art veterinary clinics across London. Her interests include internal medicine, in which she is undertaking the advance practitioner certificate, and rabbit medicine – having always had her own bunny until very recently! Dr Hollie arrived in Singapore earlier in 2015, relocating with her partner and rescue Collie-cross named Max – and since arriving has been adopted by a cheeky Singapore Special named Chelsea!

Specialty:
Vaccinations
Microchipping
Euthanasia services
General surgery
Spaying and neutering
Veterinary Chiropractic
