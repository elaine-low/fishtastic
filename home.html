About Us

Our Mission

To expand the joys and delight of fishkeeping to the general public society.

Our Vision

To be the number one fish aquarium in the worldwide fish aquarium business sector.

Who Are We?

In 2013, the co-sponsor of Singapore Sea Aquarium Mr. K. H. Chan established Fishtastic. Fishtastic offers an assortment of fishes costs ranging from moderate to extravagant prices. Fishtastic convey products of fish nourishments and accessories from many well-known brands such as Hikari, Rio and Tropical. Fishtastic has been awarded Singapore’s Outstanding Enterprise 2014 and Top Asia Enterprise 2014. Individuals from the general population has nominated us for our quality of products, social responsibility and eminence in customer service. We are grateful for the acknowledgement.

We have established trading partners managing businesses in nations everywhere throughout the world. In order to gain more products we are always exploring and searching for trading partners worldwide. By doing so we can proceed to developing our farm into a one-stop fishing haven for all fish lovers.

Come by our store to make your marine aquarium flawlessly eye catching and jaw dropping magnificent. We are here to share our insight and make your interests more enjoyable. It is really a benefit to have the opportunity to look at a part of the ocean everyday.

If you have any inquiries, please do not hesitate to get in touch with us by telephone or email.
