Goals

  • To redevelop Fish-Tastic's existing website to be more organised and visually-appealing.
  • To add new content about Fish-Tastic's new businesses.
  • To redevelop Fish-Tastic's existing website so as to facilitate e-commerce.

Success Evaluation

  • There is an increase in web-traffic to Fish-Tastic's website.
  • There is an increase in the number of customers patronising the store.
  • There is an increase in the number of customers purchasing fishes and aquarium supplies from Fish-Tastic.
  • There is a steady increase in the number of new customers who are interested in the pet shop , pet shop supplies, as well as the veterinary.
  • There is a steady increase in the number of residential and corporate customers who are interested in landscaping and aquascaping classes.
  • There is an increase in the number of event-goers attending the events organised by Fish-Tastic.
  • There is an increase in the amount of sales for Fish-Tastic.

Target Audience

Target Audience: Young Adults and Working Adults (Ages 18 to 40)

How to cater: We are providing relevant information in regards to their interest in animals

    • Landscaping Competition
      Design a landscape and fish pond as creative as you can by providing a 300 word description of the landscape along with hand drawn or computer drawn designs. Last date for submission is on the 30th April 2016.
    • Fish Forum
      This is the perfect place if you want to discuss about seawater or freshwater fishes, types of aquarium tanks and types of fish products. If you are interested come by for the forum on the 5th May 2016.
    • Fish Exhibition
      One day international scientific fish exhibition organized by Thailand Department of Fishes (DOF), Asian Fisheries Society (AFS) and Southeast Asian Fisheries Development Centre (SEAFDEC) on the 15th May 2016.
    • Landscape & Koi Pond Exhibition
      Held on 6th June 2016, GreenUrbanScape Asia is conducting a leading landscape and koi pond exhibition for urban design, greenery and landscape.
    • Vet Consultation Service
      At our new veterinary clinic we offer various ranges of services and free consultations to sick and injured animals. We will be having free consultation services on the 15th June 2016 for a wider range of services and all types of animals are welcomed.
    • Puppies Fashion Show
      Join us on 1st July 2016 at our local store for a fun day filled of puppies working the runway in our Puppies Fashion Show. Everyone is welcome to come by our store to have your puppy dressed up with cute outfits and accessories to participate in the show.
    • Pets Fashion Show
      If watching a puppy fashion show is not enough watch out for our Pets Fashion Show on the 19th July 2016. All pets are welcomed to participate in the pets fashion show. This is going to be a bigger fashion show and you most definitely would not regret watching it.

      We will be bringing in a specialized team of grooming experts to groom your pets on the 8th August 2016. Key in this important date so you do not miss out on awesome grooming services provided to treating your pets to the best pet spa to ever exist.
    • Puppy Show With Animal Planet
      Come down the store to watch videos of adorable litters of puppies performing cute fluffy acts to being clumsy in the cutest way possible all from animal planet. We will be setting up a mini theatre outside to showcase these videos with mini chairs for interested bystanders to watch on the 20th August 2016.
    • Pets Grooming Competition
      With more than $50,000 in prizes to win SuperZoo’s pet grooming competition brings out the most talented of groomers to showcase their best creation and artistic side. Register now or come spectate on the 9th September 2016.
    • Special Event With Dog Whisperer
      There is nothing more exciting than seeing the dog whisperer in the flash on the 20th September 2016.
