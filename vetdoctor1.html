Dr. Dymphna Overwijn

BVSc (Hons) Sydney
Post Grad. Dip. (Vet Clinical Studies)
MANZCVS (Small Animal Medicine)

Dr. Overwijn graduated with second class honours in 2003 from the University of Sydney. She worked in private practice in Singapore for 2 years, then made the move overseas to work for the University Veterinary Teaching Hospital in Sydney. She obtained her memberships in small animal medicine with the Australian College of Veterinary Scientists and completed a Post-Graduate Diploma in Veterinary Clinical Studies. She finally made the move back to Singapore at the end of 2007. She is currently pursuing her Masters in Veterinary Clinical Studies, focusing on retroviral diseases in the cats of Singapore.

Specialty:
Microchipping
Dental Checkup
General Surgery
Spaying and neutering
Veterinary pharmaceuticals
Rehabilitation services
Emergency Services
